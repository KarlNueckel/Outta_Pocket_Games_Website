How to Play

  1. Shuffle the Cards: Place the deck face down in the center of the table.
  2. Draw a Card: The youngest player draws the first card and places it face up next to the deck.
  3. Read It Aloud: The player who draws the card reads it out loud to the group.
  4. Deliberate and Decide: Players discuss who best fits the card. If there’s no clear choice, vote (majority wins).
       • No voting for yourself
       • No double voting
       • Reader can vote
       • Ties or skips → card goes to the bottom of the deck
  5. Score It: The chosen player keeps the card and adds its point value (1–3) to their score.
  6. Next Round: The next player (clockwise) draws a new card. Repeat steps 2–5.
  7. Win the Game: First to 10 points wins — they’re officially the most "Outta Pocket."

* If a card has already been played, vote for the second most fitting person.
